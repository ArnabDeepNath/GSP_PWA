# Privacy Policy for GST Sync

**Effective Date:** November 10, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 10, 2025

## Introduction

GST Sync ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our GST Filing and Invoice Management mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you register for and use GST Sync, we collect the following information:

- **Email Address:** Used for account authentication and communication
- **Display Name:** Optional, used to personalize your experience
- **User ID:** Automatically generated to identify your account

### Business and Invoice Data

To provide our core functionality, we collect and store:

- **Store/Business Information:** Business name, GST registration numbers, addresses
- **Party Information:** Customer and supplier names, GSTIN, addresses, contact details
- **Invoice Data:** Invoice numbers, dates, amounts, tax calculations, line items
- **Transaction Records:** Sales and purchase records for GST filing
- **CSV Files:** Data you import for bulk party or invoice creation

### Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information:

- **Device Type:** Device model and manufacturer
- **Operating System:** Android version
- **App Version:** Version of GST Sync installed

### Files and Documents

- **CSV Files:** Only files you explicitly select for import
- **PDF Files:** Invoices generated by the app that you choose to save or share
- **Export Data:** CSV files you choose to export

**Important:** We do NOT access your photos, videos, music, or other personal files without your explicit selection.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Core App Functionality

- Authenticate and secure your account
- Store and sync your GST and invoice data across devices
- Generate GST reports and tax summaries
- Create and manage invoices
- Calculate tax amounts and financial summaries
- Import and export data in CSV format
- Generate PDF invoices

### Service Improvement

- Analyze app usage to improve features and performance
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Provide customer support

### Communication

- Send important account notifications
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Notify you of app updates (with your permission)

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored

- **Firebase Cloud Firestore:** Your business and invoice data is securely stored on Google Cloud Platform servers
- **Firebase Authentication:** Your authentication credentials are managed by Firebase
- **Location:** Data is stored on servers located in secure data centers

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- **Encrypted Storage:** Data is encrypted at rest on Firebase servers
- **Authentication:** Secure Firebase Authentication protects your account
- **Access Controls:** Only you can access your data through authenticated sessions
- **Regular Updates:** We regularly update security measures to address new threats

### Data Backup

- Your data is automatically backed up by Firebase Cloud Firestore
- Backups are encrypted and stored securely
- You can export your data at any time

## Permissions and File Access

### Permissions Used

Our app requests only essential permissions:

- **Internet Access:** To sync data with Firebase and authenticate
- **Storage Access (Android 9 and below only):** To read/write CSV files you select

### Permissions NOT Used

We do NOT request or use:

- ❌ Camera access
- ❌ Microphone access
- ❌ Location access
- ❌ Contacts access
- ❌ Call logs access
- ❌ SMS access
- ❌ All files access (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)
- ❌ Photo and video access

### File Access on Modern Android (10+)

- **No permissions needed:** We use the system file picker
- **You choose files:** You explicitly select which CSV files to import
- **You choose save location:** You decide where to save exported files
- **Scoped storage:** Files are only accessible through your explicit selection
